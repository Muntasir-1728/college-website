FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common examination queries

How can I download my hall ticket?

You can download your hall ticket by entering your Student ID in the Hall Ticket section above. Make sure to download and print it at least 2 days before your first exam.

What if I lose my hall ticket?

You can re-download your hall ticket from the portal anytime. In case of emergency, contact the examination cell with your ID proof for a duplicate copy.

When will results be declared?

Results are typically declared within 3-4 weeks after the completion of examinations. You will receive an email notification once results are published.

How can I apply for revaluation?

Revaluation applications can be submitted within 10 days of result declaration. Visit the examination cell or apply online through the student portal with the required fee.

Need Help with Examinations?

Our examination cell is here to assist you with any queries regarding exams, results, or hall tickets.
