FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply for admission through our online portal or visit the admissions office in person. The application process includes filling out the form, submitting required documents, and appearing for an entrance test if applicable.

What are the fee payment options?

We accept payments through multiple modes including online banking, credit/debit cards, UPI, demand draft, and cash payment at the accounts office. EMI options are also available through select banks.

Is hostel facility available?

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities including WiFi, mess, gym, and 24/7 security. Hostel applications are processed on a first-come, first-served basis.

How do I get my transcripts?

Transcripts can be requested through the examination cell. You need to submit an application form along with the required fee. Processing typically takes 5-7 working days.

Does the college provide placement assistance?

Yes, our dedicated placement cell organizes campus recruitment drives, workshops, and training programs. We have tie-ups with leading companies across various industries.

How can I contact a specific department?

You can find department-specific contact details on our website under the 'Faculty' section, or contact the main reception desk who will connect you to the relevant department.
